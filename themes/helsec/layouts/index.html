Meetups

In our meetups you can meet people, enjoy beverages, and listen to awesome talks. Our meetups are open for everyone and are also streamed on Twitch!

Workshops

Want to learn hacking, reverse engineering, or forensics? We hold workshops where you can learn new cyber security tips and tricks.

And more

Discord server, swag, stickers, and a challenge coin for a starter. We also support Next Gen Hack Challenge, Women4Cyber Finland, and hold hacking courses for a local high school.

HelSec membership

HelSec is a registered association and welcomes membership applications. With a membership you receive the right to vote in our regular meetings, a helsec.fi email address, a challenge coin, a privilege to order HelSec swag, and an opportunity to sign up to our meetups and workshops 24 hours before others.
