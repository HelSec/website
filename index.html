And more

Discord server, swag, stickers, and a challenge coin for a starter. We also support Gen Z Challenge and hold hacking courses for a local high school.

HelSec membership

HelSec is an registered association and welcomes membership applications. With a membership you receive right to vote in our regular meetings, helsec.fi email address, a challenge coin, privielege to order HelSec swag, and opportunity to sign up to our meetups and workshops 24 hours before others.
